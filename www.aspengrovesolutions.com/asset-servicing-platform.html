Platform overview

Solutions for synchronizing mortgage servicing

Implement a platform to orchestrate & synchronize servicing operations, integrate to servicing and other systems, eliminate process breaks, and save $Millions

platform capabilities

An extensible platform with off-the-shelf modules

Borrower Engagement

Enhance relationships with borrowers and digitize servicing interactions to save time and money.  Provide homeowners with services that delight and create loyalty.

Compliance

Multi-dimensional solutions including comprehensive audit trails on every file for every change, standardized criminal background checks for mortgage field services and solutions that significantly reduce the overhead of regulatory and operational audits.

Collections

Eliminate manual checklists, reduce call volumes and eliminate after-call follow ups, enhance outcomes, reduce stress, and speed up new team member onboarding.

Loss mitigation

Automated rules and processes guide operators and borrowers to the best outcome for all parties quickly.

Bankruptcy & foreclosure

Enable end-to-end process tracking at a much lower cost that competitive products.

Inspections & preservation management

Reduce advance funding by up to 75% by only advancing what is allowed under investor rules and what is signed off. Eliminate billbacks, normalize your data across vendors, and manage SLAs and compliance.

Property registrations

Streamline registrations/de-registrations through automated integrations, management and completion tracking and support compliance across municipalities nationwide.

Hazard claims

Organize the data intensive maintenance and monitoring of hazard claims and alleviate the resource demands of gathering data and submitting claims.

Capital repairs

Oversee the complex loss draft process efficiently, Collect and review data and materials needed for speedy approval. Manage spend and timelines to preserve portfolio value.

REO

Combine the many stages of REO management into a streamlined, visible and efficient workflow. Easily assign and manage agents, vendors or outsourcers. Accelerate the sales process.
