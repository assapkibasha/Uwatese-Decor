Our people make us who we are

We’re all about our people. Our growth is a result of the strong bonds of togetherness and community spirit that we encourage and promote. We want every team member to fulfill their personal & professional goals helping us all grow stronger, together.

Why you’ll enjoy working here

First off, we’re friendly folk. We look out for each other and we’re collaborative by nature. We’re a diverse, inclusive bunch and we encourage each other to develop skills and talents through further education and on-the-job experiences. We all have the same goals—grow individually and collectively, and become the best version of ourselves.

What matters to us defines us

While our technology innovation is unique to the industry, it’s really our people that have made Aspen Grove the success it has become. From our founders, to our leadership team, to each and every member of our workforce, we all share the following values.

Ambition
Ambition
We service some of the biggest banks in America. That speaks volumes about our ambition and drive as a company and we’re always on the lookout for like-minded and motivated people. While we operate in a very team-based work environment, there is still a huge amount of autonomy in roles for those willing to take on the responsibility.
Support
Support
Support comes as second nature to us—from supporting clients through their challenges, or welcoming new hires into the fold, or simply rolling up our sleeves and helping each other out. Nobody is cast adrift and left alone to deal with problems. We buddy up new hires with experienced colleagues to train them in and have weekly 1-to-1 catch ups with managers. All of us endeavor to help, take ownership, show leadership and look out for each other.
People
People
We are a very human-centered company. We hire for attitude, as much as aptitude, and have fun and enjoy each other's company. Everyone plays their part in the continued success of Aspen Grove. Some of us have been here for over 20 years. We all feel a great sense of pride in positively impacting the lives of our clients and their homeowners.
Enjoyment
Enjoyment
We have nurtured a friendly team-oriented environment and believe in a healthy work-life balance. We all get along, and enjoy regular get-togethers with our sports and social club. From online and offline activities, nights at the races, DJ parties, family open days and BBQs, we like to let our hair down on occasion and have a bit of craic.
New learning
New learning
Nothing stands still in this industry. We embrace new challenges and take on new ideas and insights. There is always something new to learn and the opportunity to get involved with lots of varied projects. We encourage all our team members to develop and better themselves, learn new perspectives and further their professional education. There is no finishing line here, just steps forward.
